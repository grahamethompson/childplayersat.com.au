School Holiday Program

This two-week full time school holiday drama program and production has been running in January for several years with the last production being the very popular Peter Pan in January this year.

We are planning to continue the program in January 2014 with a production of The Jungle Book.

This two-week school holiday drama program is unique in that all the children get the chance to perform on stage in a proper theatre with 8 stage performances.

The program runs from 10.00-4.00 pm each day. The first week of the program is run at the Downer Community Centre and the second week, and performances, will be at Theatre,Ellery Crescent, Acton. This program is suitable for children aged 8-16 and no singing or acting experience is required. The musical production of The   Jungle Book which will be performed in January 2014 is a new production written by BJ Anyos, our Artistic Director, and Georgia Pike the Musical Director. There are roles suitable for all age groups and experience levels. The children will be supported on stage by experienced teen actors who have worked with the program in previous years. The production lasts one hour and we are sure will be enjoyed by participants and audiences alike.
